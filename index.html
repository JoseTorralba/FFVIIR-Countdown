Story

The world has fallen under the control of the Shinra Electric Power Company, a shadowy corporation controlling the planet’s very life force as mako energy.

In the sprawling city of Midgar, an anti-Shinra organization calling themselves Avalanche have stepped up their resistance. Cloud Strife, a former member of Shinra’s elite SOLDIER unit now turned mercenary, lends his aid to the group, unaware of the epic consequences that await him.

A spectacular re-imagining of one of the most visionary games ever, the first game in this project will be set in the eclectic city of Midgar and presents a fully standalone gaming experience.

Characters

Cloud Strife

The main character in Final Fantasy VII. Former member of SOLDIER, is now a mercenary who will take any job. He gradually gets caught up in a struggle for the life of the planet.

Aerith Gainsborough

Young, beautiful, and mysterious, Aerith met Cloud while selling Flowers on the streets of Midgar. She decided to join him soon after. Her unusual abilities enable her to use magic.

Tifa Lockheart

Bright and optimistic, Tifa always cheers up the others when they're down. she can decimate almost any enemy with her fists. She is one of the main members of AVALANCHE.

Barret Wallace

Leader of AVALANCHE, Barret is fighting against Shinra. Barret depends on brute strength and his "Gun-arm" to see him through. Currently lives with his daughter Marlene.
