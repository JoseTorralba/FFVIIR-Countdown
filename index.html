Story

Mako, the lifeblood of the planet and the fuel that powers the city of Midgar...

By exploiting this resource through their Mako Reactors, the Shinra Electric Power Company has all but seized control of the entire world. A ragtag group of idealists, known as Avalanche, are one of the last bastions of resistance.

Cloud Strife, a former member of Shinraâ€™s elite SOLDIER unit now turned mercenary, lends his aid to the group, to destroy Mako Reactor 1 in the city of Midgar.

The bombing plunges the city into fiery chaos, and amidst the raging inferno, Cloud is tormented by visions of a bitter enemy long thought dead.

Once more begins a story that will shape the destiny of an entire world.

Characters

Cloud Strife

The main character in Final Fantasy VII. Former member of SOLDIER, is now a mercenary who will take any job. He gradually gets caught up in a struggle for the life of the planet.

Aerith Gainsborough

Aerith lives in the Sector 5 slums, who first meets Cloud on the streets of Midgar. Shinra has kept a close eye on her ever since she was a child, as she has the ability to hear the voice of the planet.

Tifa Lockheart

A loyal member of Avalanche, an underground organization opposed to Shinra. Tifa also runs Seventh Heaven, a popular bar in the Sector 7 slums.

Barret Wallace

Leader of AVALANCHE, Barret is fighting against Shinra. Barret depends on brute strength and his "Gun-arm" to see him through. Currently lives with his daughter Marlene.

Sephiroth

Sephiroth is the main antagonist of Final Fantasy VII. Sephiroth is a former SOLDIER. Learning of his true origins drove him insane, causing him to be driven by a desire to destroy the world.

Reno

Reno is a prominent member of the Turks. Often accompanied by fellow Turk, Rude, Reno wields an Electro-Mag Rod in battle, a nightstick with a taser on one end.

Rude

Rude is a member of the Turks. Rude is rarely seen without his long-time partner Reno and is taciturn, tending to relegate the talking to Reno. He prefers to use his fists in battle.

Red XIII

Red XIII is a beast that can talk as humans can. Imprisoned as an experiment sample by Shinra's Science Division, he has been categorized with a number rather than his real name.
